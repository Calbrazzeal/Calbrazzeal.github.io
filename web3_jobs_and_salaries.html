Web3 Jobs and Salaries

Web3 is the next generation of the internet, powered by blockchain and decentralized technologies. It opens up new opportunities for developers, entrepreneurs, and innovators who aspire to create and utilize applications that prioritize security, transparency, and fairness.

Web3 jobs encompass a wide spectrum, ranging from blockchain developers to crypto analysts. Average salaries for these roles span from $60,000 to $300,000 per year.
